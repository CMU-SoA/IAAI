IA:AI STUDIO // SPRING '22

PITTSBURGH STEPS

Phase 1 - Precedent Research & Analysis

The first phase in the project looked at The Highline Park in Manhattan, New York as the primary subject of precedent research before looking at the Pittsburgh stpes as sites for research and design.

Phase 2 - Site Mapping and Research

The second phase in the project was focused on analyzing and mapping the Pittsburgh steps as part of the city's circulation to identify opportunities for urban revitalization. We formed teams of two, and each team focused on one Pittsburgh neighborhood.

Phase 3 - Field Research

The third phase in the project allows each team to engage in hands-on work by choosing one of three issues (construction, ecology, and experience) to develop further research material regarding the current state of the Pittsburgh steps.
